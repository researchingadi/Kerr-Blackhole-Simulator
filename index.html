Cloud Disk Mode

Interaction: Click launches photon trajectories; Shift+Click injects matter into the accretion flow.
Physics: Schwarzschild null-ray geodesic with Kerr frame dragging (Lense-Thirring).
Disk: Blackbody T(r), relativistic Doppler δ=√(1-β²)/(1-β cosθ), beaming I∝δ³·⁴.
Particles: Keplerian orbits ω∝r⁻³/², Doppler-colored. Click to add bursts.
Photon Lab: Press P for null-ray paths; M shows horizon/photon-sphere/ISCO measurement rings.
Split Mode: Press X to compare no-lensing vs full gravitational lensing in real time.
Mission: Press Enter for a guided demo sequence with photon launch, disk flare, and final orbit.
Observatory: Press O for documentary-style rendering with restrained bloom, volumetric disk haze, and subtle auto exposure.
Telescope: Press T for a clean telescope-capture view with UI suppressed and realism tuned.
Score: Press N for an original organ/space ambience, or L to load a local audio file you own.
Traverse: Press 6 for a low-altitude glide above the disk with volumetric plasma haze, thicker cloud layers, and close-range detail.
